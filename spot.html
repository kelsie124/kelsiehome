Shakuji Koen

Located in Tokyo, great for picnic with friends and family. Green spaces and lake, beautiful cherry blossoms in spring, ideal for nature lovers and peaceful escape.

Oizumi Gakuen

The birthplace of Anime. This area steeped in anime history and culture, must visit place for anime enthusiast. Many good restaurants around here too, my go to is Butcher's Table burger - well known for featured in Your Lie in April anime.
